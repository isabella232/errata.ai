Introducing Vale Server

Your style, our editor

Vale Server is a cross-platform (, , and ) desktop application that brings your editorial style guide to life. Get Started Documentation
  • Native Performance

    Vale Server is a native desktop application. There are no size restraints, usage quotas, or other limitations.

  • Extensible

    Vale Server is 100% compatible with the Vale command-line tool, allowing you to leverage its ecosystem of styles.

  • Private & Secure

    Vale Server runs on entirely on localhost—ensuring that your content is never sent to a remote server for processing.

NOT ANOTHER GRAMMAR CHECKER

Control your content

Vale Server doesn’t attempt to teach you how to write; it’s a tool for writers. It focuses on writing style—allowing you to control your voice, terminology, branding, and more.
  • Choose your style

    Styles are folders consisting of simple, easy-to-share YAML files. You can create your own or use one from our existing collection.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How does payment work?

    Your payment is handled securely by Gumroad (see Safe buying on Gumroad). Your data never touches our servers.

  • How do I retrieve a lost license key?

    Please use the license key lookup service. For trial licenses, please send an email from the address associated with your license.

  • What are the system requirements?
    • macOS: High Sierra (10.13) and newer releases are supported.
    • Windows: Windows 10 systems are supported.
    • Linux: All major distributions are supported through an AppImage.
  • What is your update policy?

    Existing license holders receive all bug fixes and same-version feature updates.

    Major version releases (for example, v1.x.x to v2.0.0) may require an upgrade.

  • What is your refund policy?

    We do not offer refunds. Instead, we offer a 30-day, zero-obligation free trial to allow you to evaluate Vale Server prior to buying.

  • Where do I ask support questions?

    Support is available through both GitHub issues and email (support@errata.ai). You'll typically receive a response in less than 24 hours.
